The Interrupters Fan Page



Bio:

This is a fan page for a great ska punk band that is still making noise. For a while there has been a lack of good punk ska, heck... even 3rd wave ska has come and gone with little to show for it. This high intensity band is worth a listen for anyone who enjoys some great high energy fun! Please take a listen and enjoy! You can check out there website HERE for tour dates and and merch stuff.


The Band:

  • Aimee Interrupter: Lead Vocals
  • Kevin Bivona: Guitar, Lead and Backing Vocals
  • Justin Bivona: Bass and Backing Vocals
  • Jesse Bivona: Drums and Backing Vocals
